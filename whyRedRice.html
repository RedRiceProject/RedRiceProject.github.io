Why RedRice?

Not only is RedRice a very healthy way to add iron and vitamin b12 into your diet, but it is also largely harmless. This is because the only difference between RedRice and your normal everyday rice is the addition of iron and vitamin b12. This means that the only potential risk is iron overdose. This is hardly a concern because the amount of iron needed to be consumed to see these symptoms are much higher than an average daily intake of rice. The recommended daily intake of iron is approximately 7 mg and RedRice will be able to provide to that need.
