About RedRice

Iron And Vitamin B12

These are the two biggest deficiencies in people all around the world, which can cause major Anemia. If we can supplement these to people who need it, we can save millions.

How is RedRice Created?

RedRice is a genetically modified organism. To create Vitamin B12 and Leghemoglobin, the easiest digestible form of Iron, the rice takes genes from soybeans and Pseudomonas Aeruginosa, a bacteria.We can isolate the genes required to produce Vitamin b12 and leghemoglobin and introduce them to rice, creating a crop whose seeds can be sold commercially worldwide to countless farmers in areas around the world. For the most at-risk populations, partnership with charitable relief organizations such as the International Committee of the Red Cross can allow road convoys or airdrops of bags of the crop can be employed.
