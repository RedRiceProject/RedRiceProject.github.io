About RedRice

Iron And Vitamin B12

These are among the most prevalent nutrient deficiencies worldwide and are the primary causes of the anemia crisis. If we can supplement these nutrients to those in need, we can save millions of lives.

How is RedRice Created?

RedRice is a genetically modified organism. Vitamin B12 and Leghemoglobin (an easily digestible source of Iron) are produced in the rice by the addition of genes from Glycine Max and Pseudomonas Aeruginosa (soybeans and a specific bacteria). After we isolate these genes and introduce them to rice, the GM (genetically modified) seeds can be sold to countless farmers in areas around the world. For the most at-risk populations, partnership with charitable relief organizations such as the International Committee of the Red Cross can employment road convoys or airdrops of bags of the crop.
